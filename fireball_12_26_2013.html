FIREBALL 12-26-2013....

The big Iowa fireball on 12-26-2013....This was at 5:41pm.... This was captured by my 2 meteor cams....

This was a very large event seen across 10 states and Canada.... Reports came in from over 700 people who saw the event.... This was a very exciting time to be tracking fireball meteors....

In the image below,,the fireball is the bright streak in the upper right corner,,just above the horizon....This would be north towards Des Moines....
